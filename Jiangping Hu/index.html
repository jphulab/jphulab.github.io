Principal Investigator

Jiangping Hu
Professor, Doctoral Supervisor
University of Electronic Science and Technology of China
School of Automation Engineering
Center for Biological Intelligence and Control
hjp_lzu@163.com

Research Interests:

  • Modeling and control of complex systems, Distributed control and optimization, Intelligent sensing and decision, Optimization of hydrogen
    energy systems.

Academic activities:

  • IEEE Senior Member, Editorial Board members of Journal of Systems Science and Complexity, Kybernetika, Mathematics, PLOS Complex Systems, Member of the Control Theory Committee of the Chinese Society of Automation, member of Chinese Institute of Command and Control, member of Chinese Mathematical Society.

Academic achievements:

  • Published 120 SCI papers and 16 ESI papers in system control journals such as IEEE TAC and Automatica. He has won the “Most Cited Article” in Automatica in 2010, the “Best Paper Award” in Control Theory and Technology in 2021, the Wiley Open Science Excellent Author Program in 2023, Best Paper Award of Journal of Systems Science & Complexity in 2025, Outstanding Paper Award of Control Theory and Technology in 2025. He has applied for more than 10 invention patents and has authorized 8. Editing “Modeling, Analysis and Control of Multi-Agent Systems” (National Defense Industry Press, July 2019). He has undertaken important scientific research projects such as the national key research and development projects, the National Natural Science Foundation Projects and the New Century Excellent Talents in University. He has been selected into the list of the top 2% scientists in the world (from 2020 to present) and the top 100,000 scientists in the global scholar library.
